LASAÑA DE TOFU CREAM Y CALABACÍN

At home you can never eat pasta, I do not know what it is but it is always well received by our palates and stomachs hehehe ... So let's try new things experimenting with what there is at home and in an improvisation morning, this delicious lasagna of tofu-crema with zucchini and tomato emerged. He baptized the title with the term tofu-crema because I am very imaginative when I want, jurjur ... No, he called it that because he passed it through the raw processor and he got an unctuous consistency pâté type that has been super good to bind all the vegetable sauce.
